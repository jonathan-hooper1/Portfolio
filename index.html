About me

I'm a finance graduate who can't leave well enough alone. If something doesn't make sense to me, I dig until it does — then I figure out how to explain it to someone else in a way that actually sticks.

At Benchmark I converted cold-door prospects into paying customers in demanding outdoor conditions — high rejection, no script safety net, and results that depended entirely on how fast I could read someone and adapt.

At Crossbow Marketing I built outbound campaigns from scratch — Gmail sequences, GoHighLevel workflows, and a sales team I recruited and trained from zero. I used AI to analyze response data and continuously refine our messaging until the numbers moved.

At Putnam Automotive I executed targeted outbound calls to re-engage lapsed customers and was awarded a bonus for the highest number of appointments scheduled across March, April, and May 2021.

I'm based in Austin, TX and looking for roles where analytical thinking and clear communication both matter.
